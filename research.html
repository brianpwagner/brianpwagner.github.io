Research Areas

Superconducting Microelectronics

Dielectric film development, superconducting resonator integration, and materials characterization for SCE logic technologies. Foundational work supporting Nb-based superconductor devices and qubit fabrication.

Phase-Change RF Switches

World-record chalcogenide-based RF switching technology (GeTe) achieving 15 THz cutoff frequency. Enabled reconfigurable defense Tx/Rx systems, antennas, and filters.

Wide Bandgap Semiconductors

Growth and characterization of GaN, AlN, SiC, and solid-solution (SiC)x(AlN)1-x materials. Pioneering work in non-polar GaN via pendeo-epitaxy for high-power and high-frequency devices.

Acousto-Optic & Nonlinear Optics

Crystal growth and device development for acousto-optic tunable filters and nonlinear optical materials including orientation-patterned ZnSe and selenide compounds for MWIR/LWIR applications.

Radiation & IR Detectors

Novel semiconductor materials for room-temperature gamma-ray detection (DHS/DNDO sponsored), PbSe thin-film IR detectors, and nano avalanche photodiode architectures for single-photon detection.

Crystal Growth & Thin Films

Physical vapor transport (PVT) growth of SiC, CVD of 3C-SiC on Si, PbSe thin films, and germanium/silicon thin-film dielectrics for quantum computing and advanced semiconductor applications.
